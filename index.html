About Me

I am a Senior Lecturer and Research Coordinator in the School of Information Technology at Whitecliffe College, Wellington. I hold a PhD in Computer Science from Victoria University of Wellington, where my doctoral research focused on combinatorial optimisation and metaheuristic algorithms.

My current research spans three interconnected areas: combinatorial optimisation (particularly Tabu Search and Iterated Local Search for problems such as the Quadratic Assignment Problem and portfolio selection), applied machine learning for social impact, and an emerging programme in explainable AI for clinical cancer survivorship research in collaboration with networks across New Zealand and the University of Leeds.

I also serve as a Senior AI Consultant with AIlogy Vietnam and Dude.ai Vietnam, bridging academic research and industry applications of artificial intelligence.
