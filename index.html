About Me

I am a Senior Lecturer in the School of Information Technology at Whitecliffe College, Wellington. I hold a PhD in Computer Science from Victoria University of Wellington, where my doctoral research focused on combinatorial optimisation and metaheuristic algorithms.

My current research spans three interconnected areas: combinatorial optimisation , applied machine learning for social impact (Vietnamese educational content safety, banking peformance, business sentimental analysis), and an emerging programme in explainable AI for clinical cancer survivorship research in collaboration with networks across New Zealand and the University of Leeds.

I also serve as a AI Consultant with AIlogy Vietnam and Dude.AI, bridging academic research and industry applications of artificial intelligence.
